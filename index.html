More of About Pomace Pulp

Pulp made from wood cause deforestation and global warming and wood pulp production process is complicated and have high chemical consumption. Our Objective

Our objective is to commercialize and supply high quality pulp products to paper industries in Ethiopia and expand its supply to all African countries. The company replace imported pulp with local quality pulp (pomace pulp) and facilitate the production of high quality products.
